What ShadowOps Does (and Doesn't Do)

✓ What It Does

  • Scores job risk in real-time (which orders will slip?)
  • Detects constraints (material, labor, machine, schedule)
  • Explains root causes in plain language
  • Recommends non-destructive actions
  • Routes alerts to the right roles
  • Maintains a complete audit trail

✗ What It Doesn't Do

  • Automatically reschedule your jobs
  • Replace your ERP or MES
  • Change data without user approval
  • Make promises it can't keep
  • Add complexity to your workflows
  • Require a 6-month implementation

Core Capabilities for Faster Decisions

Risk Scoring

Per-job and per-work-center risk scores updated continuously so teams know what is likely to slip next.

Constraint Detection

Automatic identification of material shortages, labor gaps, machine utilization, and schedule conflicts without heavy setup.

Root-Cause Narrative

Plain-language explanations of why a job is at risk, so operators can act without decoding complex dashboards.

Recommendations

Suggestions for procurement, rescheduling, or mitigation that your team reviews and approves.

Role-Based Alerts

Production managers, procurement, and customer service each get role-specific alerts.

Audit Trail

Complete record of alerts, recommendations, and actions for operational accountability.

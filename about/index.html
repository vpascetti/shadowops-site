The Story

Why We Built It

After years in ERP implementations and plant operations projects, one pattern kept repeating: the data existed, but risk surfaced too late for teams to react without firefighting.

Operations leaders and executives needed a simpler layer above existing systems that could surface risk early, explain the root cause, and quantify financial exposure.

  • ERP data is available but hard to translate into early risk signals and financial exposure
  • Constraint impact is often discovered after schedules are already broken
  • Teams spend too much time reacting and not enough time preventing
  • Decision accountability is inconsistent across plants and leadership workflows

ShadowOps was designed to solve that exact gap: make late-job exposure visible early and turn it into accountable action.

Our Mission

See Risk Earlier

Before your customer feels the pain, you know what will slip. Early visibility means early action.

Know Why

Not just "Job A is late." The exact constraint: material, labor, machine, or queue. Plain language, not cryptic dashboards.

Know What to Do Next

Recommendations you control. Audit trails for accountability. Decisions tied to operational and financial impact.

Accountable Execution

Real Implementation

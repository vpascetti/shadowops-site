Security by Design.

ShadowOps is built for operational clarity without adding unnecessary data access or replacing the systems you already trust.

Read-only by default

ShadowOps surfaces risk and exposure insights without writing back into your ERP workflows.

Least-privilege connectivity

Access is scoped to only what is required for visibility, with controls aligned to your team.

Customer-controlled deployment

Run ShadowOps self-hosted, hybrid, or in enterprise cloud based on your security model.

No ERP replacement

ShadowOps sits above your ERP to improve visibility, not to replace systems of record.

Data minimization

Only the operational data needed to detect risk and quantify financial exposure is processed.
